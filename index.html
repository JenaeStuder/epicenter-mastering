Epicenter Mastering is a fully analog mastering studio, specializing in a uniquely crisp sound that is not widely available. We use only high quality analog gear but, if needed, we have plenty of premium digital plug ins.

Epicenter Mastering is located in Nashville, Tennesee on the legendary music row in the historic RCA Studio A building.


Epicenter Mastering was started by Mastering engineer Gentry Studer in 2015.
